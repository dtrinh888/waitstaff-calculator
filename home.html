The Waitstaff Calculator app computes meal costs as well as track their tips over the course of a shift.

Instructions

  • User enters the base cost of meal, tax rate and tip percentage the customer wants to leave.
  • Submit button to calculate and store the meal.
  • Cancel button to will clear "Meal Details" inputs and won't effect the "Customer Charges" or the "My Earnings Info".
  • Reset button will return app into its initial state.
